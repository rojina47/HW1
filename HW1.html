Dr Yunus indicted over labour law violation case

Published: 06 Jun 2023, 22: 22


Nobel laureate professor Muhammad Yunus File photo

A labour court in Dhaka on Monday framed charges against four people,
including Grameen Telecom chairman and Nobel Laureate Dr Muhammad Yunus,
in a case lodged over violation of labour laws.
Judge Begum Sheikh Merina Sultana of Dhaka 3rd labour court passed the order.
The other three accused in the case are - Grameen
Telecom managing director Ashraful Hasan,
board directors Nurjahan Begum and Md Shahjahan.

Ahmed took part in the hearing for Dr Muhammad Yunus.
